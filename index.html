Understanding Defence Exams

Combined Defence Services (CDS)

The Combined Defence Services (CDS) examination is conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) twice a year for recruitment into the Indian Military Academy, Indian Naval Academy, Air Force Academy, and Officers Training Academy.

Exam Pattern

  • English (100 marks)
  • General Knowledge (100 marks)
  • Elementary Mathematics (100 marks) - Except for OTA
  • 2-hour duration for each paper

Eligibility

  • Age: 19-24 years (IMA, INA, AFA); 19-25 years (OTA)
  • Educational Qualification: Bachelor's degree for IMA, INA, AFA; Bachelor's degree for OTA
  • Both men and women can apply for OTA; only men for IMA, INA, AFA

Central Armed Police Forces (CAPF)

The CAPF Assistant Commandant examination is conducted by UPSC annually for recruitment to the post of Assistant Commandant in forces like BSF, CRPF, ITBP, SSB, and CISF.

Exam Pattern

  • Paper I: General Ability and Intelligence (250 marks)
  • Paper II: General Studies, Essay & Comprehension (200 marks)
  • Physical Efficiency Test and Medical Standards Test
  • Interview/Personality Test

Eligibility

  • Age: 20-25 years
  • Educational Qualification: Bachelor's degree in any discipline
  • Physical and Medical standards as prescribed
  • Both men and women can apply
